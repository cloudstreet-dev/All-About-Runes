## Quick Divination Guide

### Reading Positions

**Upright:** Standard meaning, flow, positive expression
**Reversed (Merkstave):** Blocked, challenged, or inverted meaning

**Note:** Some runes (Gebo, Isa, Ingwaz, Dagaz) have no reversed meaning due to symmetry.

### Simple Three-Rune Spread

1. **Past/Foundation** - What brought you here
2. **Present/Challenge** - What you're facing now
3. **Future/Outcome** - Where things are headed

### Yes/No Reading

Draw one rune:
- **Positive runes:** Fehu, Gebo, Wunjo, Jera, Sowilo, Dagaz → Yes
- **Challenging runes:** Hagalaz, Nauthiz, Isa, Thurisaz → No, or "not yet"
- **Neutral runes:** Others → Depends on question/context
